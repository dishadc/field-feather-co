# Morning Warbler CTA Copy Library

Owner: CEO / Growth
Updated: 2026-04-13
Purpose: keep subscriber CTA language consistent, premium, and page-intent specific.

## Core rules
- Never use one generic newsletter line everywhere.
- Match the CTA to the reason the reader landed on the page.
- Keep tone calm, useful, and field-oriented.
- Avoid urgency tricks, pop-up language, or generic creator-economy wording.
- Offer the next useful thing, not "subscribe for updates" in the abstract.

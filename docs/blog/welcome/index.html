Welcome to the Field Notes Journal

Published April 12, 2026 · Temporary hosting infrastructure update

Field & Feather Co. now has a temporary live editorial home. Rather than wait for the permanent WordPress stack to be provisioned, we moved the journal onto GitHub Pages so publishing can start immediately and momentum does not stall.

What this unlocks

This temporary setup gives us a public URL, a stable publishing path, and a place to ship the first wave of articles while long-term hosting is finalized. That means the editorial calendar can keep moving, internal links can start forming, and article production can proceed without waiting on Cloudways, DNS, or CMS credentials.

What comes next

The Week 1 blitz is already planned: 21 articles over seven days, beginning with pillar guides on beginner birdwatching, binocular buying, and bird identification. Supporting content then expands into backyard birding, migration, regional guides, and gear comparisons.

  • Day 8 starts with three pillar articles.
  • Days 9–14 add three supporting articles per day.
  • Week 2 transitions to the standard Monday, Wednesday, Friday cadence.

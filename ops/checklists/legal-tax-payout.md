# Legal, Tax, and Payout Checklist

## Legal foundation
- [ ] Business structure selected (sole prop/LLC)
- [ ] Registered business address available for policies
- [ ] Support email and response policy published

## Tax setup
- [ ] Etsy tax settings reviewed
- [ ] Shopify tax collection configured by region
- [ ] Sales tax nexus assumptions documented
- [ ] Digital goods tax treatment verified for target states/countries

## Payout and bookkeeping
- [ ] Payout bank account connected per platform
- [ ] Currency and settlement settings reviewed
- [ ] Monthly P&L template active
- [ ] Reconciliation process defined (weekly)

## Risk controls
- [ ] No unlicensed assets in product files
- [ ] Trademark-sensitive copy reviewed
- [ ] Refund/escalation workflow documented

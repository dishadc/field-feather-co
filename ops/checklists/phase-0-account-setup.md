# Phase 0 Account Setup Checklist (Field & Feather Co.)

## Identity and infrastructure
- [ ] Domain purchased
- [ ] Brand email created (support@, hello@)
- [ ] Password manager vault created
- [ ] 2FA enabled on all platforms

## Sales channels
- [ ] Etsy seller account approved
- [ ] Shopify store created and active plan selected
- [ ] Gumroad account verified

## Fulfillment and digital delivery
- [ ] Printify account created
- [ ] Printify store connected to Etsy
- [ ] Shopify digital delivery app installed (e.g., Digital Downloads / SendOwl)

## Marketing channels
- [ ] Pinterest business account + claimed domain
- [ ] Instagram professional account
- [ ] Optional TikTok business account

## Finance and payouts
- [ ] Etsy payouts verified
- [ ] Shopify Payments/Stripe payouts verified
- [ ] Gumroad payout account verified
- [ ] Accounting destination selected (QuickBooks/Sheets)

## Compliance and policies
- [ ] Privacy policy page
- [ ] Terms of service
- [ ] Refund policy (digital goods language)
- [ ] Contact/support SLA published

## Launch gate (must pass)
- [ ] At least 20 live listings
- [ ] At least 1 successful test purchase on each active channel
- [ ] Downloads delivered with no broken links

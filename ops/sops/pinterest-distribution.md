# SOP: Pinterest Distribution Engine

## Cadence
- 3 pins/day minimum.
- 70% product pins, 20% educational birding tips, 10% brand story.

## Workflow
1. Pull next SKUs from `listings/etsy-publish-queue.csv`.
2. Use `marketing/pinterest/launch20_pin_prompts.csv` for hook + description.
3. Publish pins with UTM-tagged URL.
4. Record in `marketing/pinterest/pin_log.csv`.

## KPI targets
- Outbound CTR > 1.2%
- Save rate > 3%
- 30-day assisted revenue trend positive

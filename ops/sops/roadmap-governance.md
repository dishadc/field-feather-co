# SOP: Roadmap Governance

Canonical roadmap: `ROADMAP.md`
Owner: CEO

## Rule
The roadmap is a living CEO-owned document. It is not delegated. It is reviewed every Monday morning, updated whenever trigger events fire, and kept aligned with the product truth.

## Required structure
The roadmap must always contain:
1. North Star
2. Current Sprint (Now)
3. Up Next (Next 4–8 Weeks)
4. On the Horizon (Next Quarter)
5. Explicitly Not Building

## Monday ritual
Before major weekly execution begins:
1. Read the North Star
2. Audit Now for truth, blockers, and definition-of-done clarity
3. Re-sequence Up Next if needed
4. Reassess On the Horizon bets
5. Write/send a short roadmap brief to affected leads/workstreams

## Immediate review triggers
Do not wait for Monday if:
- a user insight contradicts a roadmap assumption
- a Now item misses definition of done
- a major competitor/platform shift occurs
- growth signals change materially
- a blocker exposes bad sequencing
- the CEO changes their mind about priority

## Quality bar
- No vague items in Now
- No roadmap bloat
- Every roadmap update should become more specific, more evidence-based, and more useful
- The roadmap must explain both what we are building and what we are explicitly not building

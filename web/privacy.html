Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

Field & Feather Co. collects only the information reasonably necessary to operate the store, fulfill orders, respond to customer support requests, and improve the business.

1. Information we may receive

We may receive order information from commerce platforms, contact details you provide when contacting support or joining a mailing list, and basic website usage data from hosting or analytics providers.

2. How we use information

We use information to deliver digital products, provide customer support, send business updates or marketing where allowed, analyze store performance, and prevent fraud, abuse, and operational errors.

3. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers required to operate the business, such as payment processors, storefront platforms, email tools, hosting providers, and analytics tools.

4. Data retention

We retain records for operational, accounting, fraud-prevention, and support purposes for as long as reasonably necessary.

5. Your choices

You may contact us to request correction or deletion of information we directly control, subject to legal, tax, fraud-prevention, and platform obligations. Marketing emails can be unsubscribed from through the platform or message footer when applicable.

6. Cookies and analytics

Our site and connected platforms may use cookies or similar technologies for security, analytics, and basic site functionality.

7. Children

The business is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this policy as the business infrastructure evolves. The effective date above reflects the current version.

9. Contact

Customer support is handled through the original storefront or marketplace order channel. See the Support page for guidance.

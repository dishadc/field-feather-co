Customer care

Support

If you purchased a Field & Feather digital product and need help, start with the storefront or marketplace where you placed the order so your order details stay attached to the request.

How to get help fastest

For the fastest help, reply through the storefront, marketplace, or order channel you used for the purchase. That keeps your order history, delivery method, and purchase details attached so support can move more quickly.

Best route by issue

  • Download not received: use the order platform’s help or message thread first.
  • File will not open: include the product name, device or app used, and what happened when you tried to open it.
  • Wrong item purchased: use the original order channel so support can review the order and available options.
  • General usage question: include the product name and how you are trying to use it.

Before you contact support

It helps to have your order confirmation, the product name, and a short description of the problem ready. If the issue is technical, include the app, device, or file type involved.

Response expectations

Field & Feather aims to respond to customer issues promptly and clearly. Most digital-product issues are easiest to resolve when the original purchase channel is used from the start.

Journal and newsletter questions

For journal reading, The Morning Warbler, or general brand questions, start with the Field Notes Journal guide. It is the best place to understand how the editorial side of the brand is organized.
